CAKES

RED VELVET CAKE

AMERICAN DESSERT

    Ingredients For the Cake:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ cups vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk (room temperature)
  • 2 eggs (room temperature)
  • 2 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar

    • For the Frosting:

  • 2 packages cream cheese(softened)
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    Procedure:

  • Preheat & Prepare: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch pans.
  • Mix Batter: Combine dry ingredients and mix wet ingredients and combine with dry ingredients.
  • Bake: Divide batter into pans and bake for 25–30 minutes. Cool completely.
  • Make Frosting: Beat cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  • Assemble: Layer cakes with frosting, frost the sides, and decorate.

CHOCOLATE CAKE

AMERICAN DESSERT

    Ingredients For the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

    • For the Chocolate Buttercream Frosting:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
  • In a large bowl, mix dry ingredients.
  • Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  • Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  • Pour batter into prepared pans.
  • Bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  • Cool completely before frosting.

BLACK FOREST CAKE

GERMAN DESSERT

    Ingredients for the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 jar of cherries (about 12 ounces), drained
  • 1 cup heavy cream (for frosting)
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cherry liqueur (optional)

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
  • Mix the dry ingredients, then add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  • Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  • Pour into pans and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  • Allow to cool completely. Slice cakes in half horizontally.

    Frosting:

  • Whip the heavy cream with powdered sugar until stiff peaks form.
  • Layer cake with whipped cream and cherries. Drizzle with cherry liqueur for extra flavor.

CHEESECAKE

GREEK DESSERT

    Ingredients for the Cake:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 4 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • Optional toppings: Nutella, blueberry compote, caramel sauce, or fresh fruit

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  • Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press into the bottom of the pan to form a crust.
  • Bake the crust for 10 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  • In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  • Add eggs one at a time, beating on low speed just until combined.
  • Pour the mixture over the cooled crust and smooth the top.
  • Bake for 50–60 minutes or until the center is almost set.
  • Cool completely at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

    Topping Suggestions:

  • Spread Nutella over the chilled cheesecake for a chocolate hazelnut flavor.
  • Top with blueberry compote for a fruity twist.
  • Drizzle caramel sauce or serve with fresh fruit like strawberries or raspberries.

VANILLA CAKE

AUSTRALIAN DESSERT

    Ingredients for the Cake:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 3/4 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream (for frosting)
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar (for frosting)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (for frosting)

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
  • In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  • In a separate bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.
  • Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk to the butter mixture, starting and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until smooth.
  • Divide the batter evenly between the pans and bake for 25–30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Let the cakes cool completely before frosting.

    Frosting:

  • Whip the heavy cream with powdered sugar and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  • Spread frosting between the cake layers, on the top, and around the sides.
  • Optional: Decorate with sprinkles, fresh fruit, or edible flowers.

COFFEE CAKE

EUROPIAN DESSERT

    Ingredients for the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup heavy cream (for frosting)
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar (for frosting)
  • 1 teaspoon espresso powder (optional, for frosting)

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 8-inch round pans.
  • In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla.
  • Alternate adding the dry ingredients, coffee, and sour cream to the butter mixture, mixing until smooth.
  • Divide the batter evenly between the pans and bake for 25–30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Let cakes cool completely before frosting.

    Frosting:

  • Whip heavy cream with powdered sugar and espresso powder (if using) until stiff peaks form.
  • Spread frosting between the cake layers and over the top and sides.
  • Optional: Dust with cocoa powder or top with chocolate shavings for decoration.

BANANA CAKE

VIETNAMESE/ INDONESIAN DESSERT

    Ingredients for the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 1/2 cups mashed ripe bananas (about 3 large bananas)
  • Optional: 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or chocolate chips

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.
  • In a bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  • Cream butter, sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  • Mix in sour cream and mashed bananas until combined.
  • Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Fold in walnuts or chocolate chips if using.
  • Pour batter into the prepared pan and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Let cool completely before slicing and serving.

FRUITCAKE

EUROPIAN DESSERT

    Ingredients for the Cake:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups mixed dried fruit (raisins, cranberries, chopped apricots)
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (walnuts, pecans, or almonds)
  • Optional: 1/4 cup brandy or rum (for soaking dried fruit)

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease and line an 8-inch round pan with parchment paper.
  • If using, soak dried fruit in brandy or rum overnight. Drain before using.
  • Cream butter and brown sugar until fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  • Mix in orange juice.
  • In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until combined.
  • Fold in dried fruit and chopped nuts.
  • Pour batter into the prepared pan and bake for 75–90 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Cool completely before serving. Optional: Wrap in plastic and store for a few days to enhance flavor.

CARROT CAKE

EUROPIAN DESSERT

    Ingredients for the Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 1 cup heavy cream (for frosting)
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar (for frosting)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (for frosting)

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.

  • In a separate bowl, mix vegetable oil, sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla until smooth.
  • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined.

  • Whip heavy cream with powdered sugar and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  • Spread frosting between the cake layers and over the top and sides.

COOKIES

CHOCOCHIP COOKIES

AMERICAN DESSERT

    Ingredients for the Cookie:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg + 1 egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  • In a bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  • In another bowl, mix melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth.
  • Beat in the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla extract.
  • Gradually stir in dry ingredients until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips.
  • Scoop dough onto baking sheets and bake for 10–12 minutes or until edges are golden.
  • Cool on wire racks before serving.

PEANUT BUTTTER COOKIES

SOUTH AMERICAN DESSERT

    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Mix peanut butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla in a bowl until well combined.
  • Roll dough into balls and place on the baking sheet. Flatten with a fork in a crisscross pattern.
  • Bake for 10–12 minutes or until edges are firm. Let cool before serving.

BISCOTTI COOKIES

ITALIAN DESSERT

    Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts or dried fruit

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  • In another bowl, beat sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Gradually add dry ingredients, then fold in nuts or dried fruit.
  • Form dough into a log and bake for 25 minutes. Cool slightly, then slice into 1-inch pieces.
  • Bake slices for 10 minutes on each side until crisp.

MACARON COOKIES

ITALIAN DESSERT

    Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 3 large egg whites
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: food coloring

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 300°F (150°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  • Mix powdered sugar and almond flour. Sift to remove lumps.
  • Beat egg whites until foamy, then add sugar gradually. Beat to stiff peaks.
  • Fold dry ingredients into meringue. Add food coloring if desired.
  • Pipe circles onto baking sheets. Rest for 30 minutes to form a skin.
  • Bake for 15–18 minutes. Let cool before filling with buttercream or ganache.

THUMBPRINT COOKIES

SWEDISH DESSERT

    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup jam (any flavor)

    Procedure:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  • Cream butter and sugar. Mix in egg yolks and vanilla.
  • Add flour and salt, mixing until dough forms.
  • Roll dough into balls and place on baking sheets. Press thumb into each ball to form an indent.
  • Fill indents with jam and bake for 12–15 minutes.
  • Cool before serving.

INDIA'S SPEACIAL SWEETDISHES

GULAB JAMUN

    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 2–3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 4–5 cardamom pods, crushed
  • Oil or ghee for frying

    Procedure:

  • Mix milk powder, flour, and baking soda. Add ghee and milk to form a soft dough.
  • Roll dough into small balls.
  • Heat oil or ghee on medium-low and fry balls until golden brown.
  • Make sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, and cardamom for 5 minutes.
  • Soak fried balls in warm syrup for 2 hours before serving.

RASGULLA

    Ingredients:

  • 1 liter whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 4–5 cardamom pods, crushed

    Procedure:

  • Boil milk, then curdle it with lemon juice. Strain to separate chenna (curds).
  • Knead chenna until smooth and make small balls.
  • Boil sugar, water, and cardamom to make syrup. Add chenna balls and cook for 15–20 minutes.
  • Cool and refrigerate before serving.

GAJAR KA HALWA

    Ingredients:

  • 1 kg grated carrots
  • 1 liter milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (cashews, almonds)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder

    Procedure:

  • Cook grated carrots in milk on medium heat until the milk evaporates.
  • Add sugar and cook for 10 minutes.
  • Heat ghee in a pan and roast nuts. Add to halwa along with cardamom powder.
  • Cook until ghee separates. Serve warm.

KAJU BARFI

    Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cashews
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Silver leaf (optional)

    Procedure:

  • Grind cashews to a fine powder.
  • Make sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water to one-string consistency.
  • Add cashew powder and cardamom, and stir to form a dough.
  • Roll out the dough and cut into diamond shapes. Decorate with silver leaf if desired.

RASMALAI

    Ingredients:

  • 1 liter milk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 liter full cream milk (for rabri)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4–5 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts

    Procedure:

  • Make chenna by curdling milk with lemon juice. Knead into a smooth dough and shape into flat discs.
  • Boil sugar and water to make syrup. Cook chenna discs in syrup for 15 minutes.
  • Prepare rabri by reducing milk to half, adding sugar, cardamom, and nuts.
  • Soak chenna discs in rabri and chill before serving.

KHEER

    Ingredients:

  • 1 liter milk
  • 1/4 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped nuts
  • 1 tablespoon raisins

    Procedure:

  • Wash rice and cook in milk on low heat, stirring occasionally.
  • Add sugar, cardamom, nuts, and raisins once the rice is soft and milk thickens.
  • Cook for another 10 minutes. Serve warm or chilled.
